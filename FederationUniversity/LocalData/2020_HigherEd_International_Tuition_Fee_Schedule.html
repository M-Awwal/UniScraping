Higher Education

2020 International Student Tuition Fee Schedule

Important information

>All fees are quoted in Australian Dollars (AUD$) and apply to Federation University Australia campuses and teaching locations only

>Program and fee information applies to students studying between 1 January and 31 December 2020 (students who commenced a program prior to 1 Jan 2018 will retain their commencement year annual fee)

>Fees are held fixed for each calendar year 1 January until 31 December

>Fees are reviewed on an annual basis and may increase in the following calendar year, fee increases are applied at the beginning of each calendar year

>Equivalent Full Time Study Load (EFTSL) is a measure of study load for a full year when undertaking a program on a full-time basis (120 credit points = 1 EFTSL i.e. 8 courses x 15 credit points)

>Program annual fees are based on a full-time study load (1 EFTSL / 120 credit points) in the same calendar year

>Semester fees are based on a full-time study load (.5 EFTSL / 60 credit points) and the final tuition fee payable each semester will be determined by your course enrolments

>Semester fee is an estimate only as fees may increase in future years - the amount you must pay is the tuition fee which has been approved for the calendar year in which the semester falls

>A student commencing in Semester Two or Summer Semester will find that their total program cost will be slightly higher than if they had commenced in Semester One

>For all programs listed students will be charged an additional tuition fee where a course (subject) needs to be repeated

>International students in Australia on a student visa cannot study a full program via Online Learning (CRICOS registration is not applicable for Online Learning program enrolments)

>Students wishing to study via Flexible (Flex) or Online delivery should contact International Admissions to discuss their study options

>Programs on offer are subject to change at the discretion of the University - refer to www.federation.study.edu.au for current information on offerings, delivery locations, entry requirements etc.
